Full Stack Developer

Hi, I'm G karthik, a MERN Stack Full Stack Developer

I specialize in building modern, scalable web applications using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js). With a focus on creating dynamic, responsive user interfaces and efficient, scalable backend solutions, I bring your ideas to life through every layer of development. From designing intuitive frontend experiences with React to implementing RESTful APIs and databases with Node.js, Express, and MongoDB, I ensure that every project I work on is both technically robust and user-centric.

Whether you're looking to develop a full-featured web application, optimize your tech stack, or build something from scratch, I am here to help you bring your vision to reality. Dive into my portfolio below to see my work, and feel free to reach out if you'd like to collaborate!
